Strategy positioning

策略定位

AURORA R1 combines RSI turning-point entries, time-separated layers, price-spaced orders and basket-level exits. It is built for active participation in two-way gold volatility.

AURORA R1将RSI极值拐头、时间分层、价格间隔开单与组合级退出整合在一起，重点是在黄金双向波动中保持可执行的交易频率。

01

Extreme-zone entry

极值区拐头入场

Sell when RSI reaches overbought and turns down; buy when RSI reaches oversold and turns up.

RSI进入超买区并向下拐头时做空；进入超卖区并向上拐头时做多。

02

Fixed-lot layering

固定手数分层

Every order uses the same base lot. New layers require a bar interval and renewed RSI extremes.

每笔订单使用相同基础手数；新层必须满足K线间隔和RSI再次进入极值区。

03

Price-spaced execution

价格间隔执行

Orders inside each layer trigger sequentially at fixed price intervals.

同层订单按照固定价格间隔逐笔触发。

04

Weighted-average TP

加权均价止盈

After multiple entries, the EA recalculates weighted average price and synchronizes basket TP.

多笔持仓后重新计算加权均价，并同步组合止盈价格。

05

Multi-path profit control

多路径盈利管理

Price TP, basket money target, dynamic target, trailing profit and partial close can operate together.

点数止盈、组合金额止盈、动态目标、追踪盈利和分批止盈可以共同工作。

06

Cycle reset

交易循环重置

After the basket closes, direction, layer and profit-tracking states reset.

组合平仓后，方向、层级及盈利跟踪状态全部重置。

Operating boundaries

Critical risk disclosure

核心风险提示

AURORA R1 is not a multiplier martingale, but it can accumulate up to 15 fixed-lot positions and has no per-order stop-loss in the delivered logic. The $10,000 basket stop is only a default parameter value, not proof of safety. Forward-test before live deployment.

AURORA R1不是倍数马丁，但最多可累积15笔固定手数订单，交付逻辑中没有逐单止损。默认10,000美元组合止损只是参数值，不等于安全。正式实盘前必须进行前向测试。
