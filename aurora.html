AURORA R1 uses the same base lot for every order. It does not multiply lot size. However, total exposure increases as layers accumulate, so prolonged one-way markets can create material drawdown.

Core architecture

核心架构

The system combines an RSI turning-point entry with time-separated layers, price-separated orders and basket-level exits.

系统将 RSI 极值拐头入场、时间分层、价格分单与组合级退出整合为一个完整交易循环。

01

Extreme-zone entry

极值区入场

A sell cycle is prepared when RSI reaches the overbought area and turns down. A buy cycle is prepared when RSI reaches the oversold area and turns up.

RSI进入超买区并向下拐头时启动空头循环；进入超卖区并向上拐头时启动多头循环。

02

Staged fixed-lot exposure

固定手数分层建仓

Each order uses the same base lot. New layers require both a bar interval and a renewed RSI extreme condition.

每笔订单使用相同基础手数。开启新层必须同时满足K线间隔和RSI再次进入极值区。

03

Price-spaced execution

价格间隔执行

Orders inside a layer are triggered sequentially at fixed price intervals, rather than being sent simultaneously.

同一层内的订单按照固定价格间隔逐笔触发，而不是一次性同时开出。

04

Weighted-average exit

加权均价退出

After multiple entries, the EA recalculates the weighted average price and synchronizes take-profit targets around the basket average.

出现多笔持仓后，EA重新计算加权持仓均价，并围绕组合均价同步更新止盈价格。

05

Multi-path profit management

多路径盈利管理

Fixed TP, basket money target, dynamic money target, trailing basket profit and partial close can work together.

固定点数止盈、组合金额止盈、动态金额目标、组合追踪止盈和分批止盈可共同管理退出。

06

Cycle reset

循环重置

When the basket is fully closed, layer counters, direction state and profit-tracking state are reset for the next cycle.

组合全部平仓后，层级、方向和盈利跟踪状态全部重置，等待下一轮信号。

Operating boundaries

运行边界

What the system is designed to do

系统擅长处理的环境

  • Frequent two-way price rotation
  • Mean-reverting or range-dominant phases
  • Markets where staged entries can improve basket average
  • Users who understand floating drawdown and fixed-lot exposure
  • 多空双向反复波动
  • 均值回归或震荡占优的阶段
  • 分层入场能够改善组合均价的行情
  • 理解浮亏和固定手数累积敞口的使用者

What it cannot eliminate

系统无法消除的风险

  • Extended one-way trends
  • Gap, slippage and execution rejection
  • Broker spread and symbol-specification differences
  • Drawdown caused by accumulated exposure
  • 持续时间较长的单边趋势
  • 跳空、滑点和交易请求失败
  • 经纪商点差与品种规格差异
  • 持仓累积造成的组合浮亏

Critical risk disclosure

核心风险提示

AURORA R1 does not use a lot multiplier, but it can hold up to 15 fixed-lot positions. It has no per-order stop-loss in the delivered logic. The default $10,000 basket stop is only a parameter value—not proof of safety and not a suitable limit for every account. Forward-test with the intended broker, symbol, timeframe and capital before live deployment.

AURORA R1 不使用倍数手数，但最多可累积15笔固定手数订单；交付逻辑中没有逐单止损。默认10,000美元组合止损只是参数值，不等于安全，也不适合所有账户。正式实盘前必须使用目标经纪商、目标品种、目标周期和计划资金进行模拟或小资金前向测试。

Deployment rules

部署规则

Keep the validated timeframe

保持验证周期一致

The EA reads the current chart timeframe. Changing timeframe changes RSI signals and the real duration of the 12-bar layer interval.

EA读取当前图表周期。更换周期会改变RSI信号，也会改变12根K线层间隔所代表的实际时间。

Use a unique Magic Number

使用独立魔术码

Do not share the same symbol and Magic Number with another EA unless joint order management is intentional.

除非明确需要共同管理订单，否则不要让其他EA在同品种使用相同魔术码。

Scale by exposure, not hope

按最大敞口评估手数

Evaluate risk based on the maximum 15-order structure, not only the first 0.01-lot entry.

评估风险时应按最多15笔订单计算，而不是只看首笔0.01手。
